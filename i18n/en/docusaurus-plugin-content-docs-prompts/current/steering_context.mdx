## 🎯 Context for Guiding

Specialized prompt for guiding and steering. Includes:

- **Steering documents** - principles of effective guidance
- **Guiding methodology** - processes of direction and steering
- **Requirements phases** - guidance through requirements
- **Design phases** - steering of architectural decisions
- **Tasks phases** - guidance of tasks and resources
- **Steering documents** - standards and procedures of guidance

**Use when:** You need to guide the project, steer the team or make guiding decisions.
